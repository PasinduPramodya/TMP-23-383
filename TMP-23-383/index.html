Motorbike Defect Checker and Predictor

The "Motorbike Defect Checker and Predictor" app is a valuable tool designed to enhance the maintenance and ownership experience of motorbike enthusiasts. This app serves as an all-in-one solution to help users efficiently identify potential defects and predict maintenance needs for their motorbikes.

Our services

How to identify altered parts in a motorbike fixed except original parts?

  1. Capture images of the original parts and altered versions.

  2. Train the ML model using the data set.

  3. Compare the images captured with the images in our trained models.
    Color pre-processing.
    Conversion to binary image.

  4. Provide an output regarding the alternation.

How to identify defects of an engine by the sound of the engine heard?



  1. Capture Voice of engine.
    We are going to train a voice-recognition model with sound clips of healthy motorbike engines and unhealthy ones and for the processing

  2. Analyze the sound
    The user will have to record a few voice clips of the sound of the engine to identify whether the strange noise is of these 1. Tick, tick tick, 2. Bump & grind, 3. Creepy krink, 4. Boo hiss, 6. Snap, crackle, pop.

How to identify major details of a motorbike if the details on the motor bike are worn out, rusted, not clear ?



  1. Manually enter the engine number, chassis number to the system.

  2. Capture images of the vehicle details
    Here user can capture the images of the engine number, chassis number

  3. Compare the images of captured with OCR models
    Color pre-processing
    Conversion to binary image

  4. Output the accuracy of the details

How to identify Motorbike tyer usage and Life expectancy prediction system with computer vision and a machine learning model.



  1. Capture images of tyres.
    Here user can capture or upload an image of a tyre and predict usage and life expectancy.

  2. Output the usage details and Life expectancy.
